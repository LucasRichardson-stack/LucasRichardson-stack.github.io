About

I'm a Learning and Development professional focused on practical, measurable outcomes. I partner with subject-matter experts and leaders to turn complex topics into experiences learners can apply on the job—not just complete in an LMS.

Whether you're launching a new capability, refreshing compliance training, or building manager skills, I bring structure, empathy, and a bias for clarity to every project.

What I focus on

  • Instructional design

    Learning objectives, storyboards, assessments, and accessible content that meets learners where they are.

  • Facilitation

    Live and virtual sessions designed for participation, reflection, and transfer back to the workflow.

  • Program design

    End-to-end curricula—from kickoff and discovery through pilot, launch, and continuous improvement.

  • Performance consulting

    Root-cause analysis when training alone won't solve the problem; solutions tied to business goals.
