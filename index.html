About

I'm a Learning and Development professional focused on practical, measurable outcomes. I partner with subject-matter experts and leaders to turn complex topics into experiences learners can apply on the job—not just complete in an LMS.

Whether you're launching a new capability, refreshing compliance training, or building manager skills, I bring structure, empathy, and a bias for clarity to every project.

I also lead at the team and department level: hiring and developing L&D talent, setting priorities across a training portfolio, and partnering with HR and business leaders so the learning function delivers measurable impact—not just activity.

Team, department & training strategy

Beyond individual programs, I manage people, shape how the training department operates, and define what the function owns—and how success is measured.

  • Team management

    Build and coach instructional designers, facilitators, and program managers—clear roles, development plans, quality standards, and delivery rhythms that keep work moving without burnout.

  • Department management

    Run the training function day to day: intake and prioritization, capacity and vendor management, LMS administration, budgets, and cross-functional governance with HR, IT, and compliance.

  • Training department scope

    Clarify what central L&D owns versus business-owned learning, enterprise standards, and escalation paths—so stakeholders know when to engage the training team and what to expect.

  • L&D strategy

    Multi-year roadmaps tied to workforce plans: capability gaps, leadership pipelines, compliance risk, and change initiatives—with KPIs for adoption, proficiency, and business outcomes.
