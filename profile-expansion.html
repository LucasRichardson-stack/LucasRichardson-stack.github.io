Global Expansion Strategy for Profile

A 12-month plan to take an ESG-specialized Brazilian communications agency into the UK and US markets; structured to build credibility first, revenue second, and a durable local presence third.

Executive Summary

Profile is the only Brazilian B-Corp agency specializing in ESG communications. This three-phase plan, Ignite, Traction, and Embed, sequences entry so the agency earns visibility before it spends on presence, leveraging a low-cost LATAM creative team and strong sustainability affiliations to win recurring revenue in two high-growth markets.

Phase Detail

What each phase delivers

Phase 1

Ignite

Months 1–3 · ~$30K
  • Localize website and portfolio to English for UK/US audiences
  • Publish ESG case studies featuring COP, TEDx, and WWF work
  • Join B-Corp networks in both markets for credibility
  • Run greenwashing-awareness webinars
  • Amplify ESG expertise through targeted LinkedIn outreach
Phase 2

Traction

Months 4–6 · ~$50–60K
  • Hire one freelance rep each in the UK and US
  • Run ESG storytelling workshops with NGO/TED networks
  • Create a referral incentive for collaborators
  • Build COP30-themed marketing kits for export
  • Close the first one to two paying clients
Phase 3

Embed

Months 7–12 · ~$110–115K
  • Formalize partnerships and white-label with UK/US firms
  • Release two new public case studies from international work
  • Launch the "Latin ESG Creative Index" content campaign
  • Convert reps to fractional Business Development managers
  • Rent desks in NYC and London impact-focused coworking hubs

Risk

Anticipated risks & mitigation

Greenwashing scrutiny

Authenticity-first storytelling and transparent sourcing protect against accusations that follow ESG-adjacent work.

Legal & cultural complexity

Local reps and partner firms bridge regulatory and cultural gaps across two distinct markets.

Limited brand awareness

Affiliations and case studies front-load credibility before sales spend begins.

Budget overshoot

A $10K contingency and phased spend gates prevent early overcommitment.

Capital exposure

Partner investment shares the cost of market entry and reduces single-party risk.
